About Us

To Celebrate Women in Computing

The UPES ACM-W was found in April, 2015 with an aim to realize the dynamic image of women in the field of computers and information technology and to provide them with a platform to showcase their technical skills. We at UPES ACM-W try to follow in their steps to create a community where all are given equal opportunities and everyone works together in harmony. UPES ACM-W Student Chapter is reserved exclusively for women with an aim to highlight the fact that women are as competent as men in the field of technology. To achieve this, we organize various activities and events to bring out the programmer and genius within each and every individual. We strive to create a healthy environment for women that encourages them to step forward and showcase their talent.

Largest Computing Society, now at UPES

ACM-W believes that putting women's vision into effect would give way to a brighter future.

Initiatives

UPES ACM-W is a student Chapter exclusively for women, created to address the technical skills of the students pursuing Computer Science in UPES. The Chapter works towards broadening the horizons of the students, help them explore and practice their technical interests, hence creating a platform apart from the standard course-work. To work towards this goal, the Chapter organizes various events including workshops and mentorship programs with people who are related to computers and well-versed with their areas of interest.

01

SPY-C

UPES ACM-W devised this initiative to help students get acquainted to programming languages. It is a platform through which students can interact with their seniors as they get a hang of coding, along the way. Spy C is a perfect blend of coding algorithms, logic and human insights.

02

WORKSHOPS

Recognizing the importance of nurturing the various fields that intrigue our members, UPES-ACM and ACM-W host interactive technical workshops that help spread awareness, foster ingenuity and sharpen skills. We at UPES ACM and ACM-W, believe in growing holistically and being the change that we want to see in the world.

03

CODE ANYTIME

Code Anytime is an initiative augmented by UPES ACM and ACM-W in an effort to kindle a coding culture within campus. It creates an environment where everyone is provided with a platform having a diverse pool of languages, to code, learn and grow.

EXCLUSIVELY BY ACM-W

UPES ACM-W organized the Regional Conference 18’ on 13th and 14th of April 2018. The conference aims at providing a platform and a pool of opportunities to help women sharpen their skills and excel in the field of computing. It consisted of 4 enticing events, which were flagged off by Mrs Rashmi Mohan, Secretary, ACM India.

  • SUPERWOMAN 2.0 : Containing the name of a famous female personality, Superwoman 2.0 was an amalgamation of a quiz and a platform that assessed the participants’ general knowledge and coding skills.
  • DUNK-A-LOT : An appealing outdoor tournament blended with a technical quiz, for the passionate basketball enthusiasts out there.
  • TECH-XHIBITION : A two-day workshop in which the participants worked on remarkable projects, conducted in collaboration with the Google Developer Student Club, Intel and Microsoft Technical Society at the University.
  • EMINENT SPEAKER PROGRAM : The Eminent Speaker Program was presided over by Mrs Rashmi Mohan, ACM India Council Secretary, a successful technologist and an exceptional mentor. She captivated the audience with a sensational speech on experiences, hence motivating the students on how to test their limits and broaden their horizon.
